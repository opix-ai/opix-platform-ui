OPIX Bibliometric analytics

OPIX Bibliometric analysis provides you with valuable insights into the competitive landscape, technological trends, and potential opportunities. It helps you make informed decisions, allocate resources effectively, and stay ahead in your respective industries.

We utilize big data and data analytics facilitating analyses on:

  1. Research Evaluation
    We provide a wealth of citation impact metrics assisting you in evaluating the impact and productivity of your research. This is valuable for academic institutions, funding agencies, and researchers themselves to assess the quality and influence of their work.
  2. Competitive Analysis
    Bibliometric analysis provides insights on the research landscape and helps organizations understand their position relative to competitors.
  3. Collaboration Analysis
    Our analysis helps you identify potential collaborators in your activity field(s) and foster networking opportunities. This is particularly useful for researchers, institutions, and businesses looking to form partnerships, share resources, and enhance their research capabilities.
  4. Knowledge Management
    Bibliometric analysis can assist in organizing and categorizing scientific literature, making it easier to access and retrieve relevant information. This can improve knowledge management within organizations, enabling efficient decision-making and fostering innovation.
  5. Funding Allocation
    We provide insights into the impact of research, projecting it into a continuous spectrum (from expected to short-term/mid-term/long-term actual impact), aiding funding agencies in making informed decisions about resource allocation. By identifying high-impact research fields and institutions, funding can be directed towards projects with the potential for significant contributions.
  6. Trend Analysis
    We help you identify research trends and emerging areas of interest while at the same time capturing and tracking their dynamics. This is valuable for businesses seeking to stay ahead of the curve, anticipate market demands, and align their R&D efforts with future opportunities.
