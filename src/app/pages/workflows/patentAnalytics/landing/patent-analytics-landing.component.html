OPIX Patent analysis

OPIX patent analysis provides you with valuable insights into the competitive landscape, technological trends, and potential opportunities. It helps you make informed decisions, allocate resources effectively, and stay ahead in your respective industries.

We utilize big data and data analytics facilitating analyses on:

  1. Competitive Intelligence
    Patent analysis allows businesses to compare their own patent portfolio with those of their competitors. This helps you understand the position in the market and identify areas where you can improve or differentiate your company.
  2. Assessing Technological Competitiveness
    Patent analysis helps businesses assess their current technical competitiveness. By analyzing patent data, businesses can gauge their strengths and weaknesses in specific technologies and identify areas where they can enhance their competitive advantage.
  3. Policy Intelligence
    By analyzing patent data, you can gain insights into technological trends and forecast future developments. This information is valuable for strategic planning, enabling you to align your policy efforts, identify potential opportunities, and plan for potential competition based on new technologies.
  4. R&D Management
    Patent analysis can guide businesses in managing their R&D efforts. By analyzing patent data, businesses can identify gaps in the existing technology landscape, uncover areas of potential innovation, and make informed decisions about their R&D investments.
  5. Business Intelligence
    Patent analysis serves as a valuable asset for business intelligence. It allows you to assess your own technological portfolio, understand your position in a technology class or industry, and make informed decisions about your business strategies.
